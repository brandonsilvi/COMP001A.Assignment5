Our Mission

The OPI was founded in June of 2010 in response to the fallout of the Deepwater Horizon oil spill. Our mission is to protect and restore the ocean's ecosystems through research, advocacy, and education.
Additionally, our team of Quality Assurance professionals perform critical testing and inspection on all offshore oil rigs in the Gulf of Mexico to ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations, helping to prevent future disasters.
